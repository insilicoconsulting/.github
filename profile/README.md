# InSilico Consulting AB

**Bioinformatics and Data Services and Support.**

We are a bioinformatics and data consultancy and contract research organization (CRO) based in Medicon Valley, which encompasses Copenhagen (Denmark), Malmö and Lund (Sweden).

With over a decade of combined industry and academic experience we provide expert hands-on support and advice to our clients, specializing in genomics and other omics modalities, clinical bioinformatics, oncology, precision medicine, and biomedical data science.
